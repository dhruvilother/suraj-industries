About Us

Suraj Industries

As a leading producer of superior quality texturized yarn and knitted fabrics, Suraj Industries takes great pride in our integrated unit which focuses on delivering excellence under one roof. Not only do we have a state-of-the-art facility located in the beautiful city of Silvassa, but our commitment to producing only the best has made us a trusted partner for a wide range of requirements.

Through the use of cutting-edge circular jacquard machines, we are able to offer innovative solutions and cater to diverse demands in the textile industry. Our strong focus on quality, efficiency and innovation has enabled us to build a reputation for excellence all across India. Whether you need custom fabrics or specialized yarn, Suraj Industries is your reliable choice for delivering superior value and quality.
